Pokemond Battle

How to Play

  1. There are two players. The player whose turn it is throws a pokeball. Here is the basic rules of each pokeball they can open:
    • If a player throw a Jigglypuff it's singing will make you fall asleep which results in -1 point.
    • If a player throw a Pickachu they get electrocuted which results in -2 points.
    • If a player throw a Snorlax it’s hug will make you happy which results in +2 points.
    • If a player throw a Charmander it’s flames will help keep you warm which results in +3 points.
    • If a player throw a Mew it will grant you any wishes which results in +5 points.
  2. After each throw, the current player can either throw again or pass it to the other player.
  3. Becareful of minus points, you can pass you turn if you fear you might loose points.
  4. Some pokemon may appear right after another.
  5. The first player to get 15 points or higher wins.
